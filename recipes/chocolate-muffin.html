Chocolate Muffin

Eggless Recipe

Ingredients

  • All purpose flour (maida) | 1 ½ cups
  • Sugar | 1 cup
  • Unsweetened cocoa powder | 2 tbsp
  • Baking powder | 1 tsp
  • Baking soda | ½ tsp
  • Salt | ⅛ tsp
  • Oil | ¼ cup
  • White distilled vinegar | 1 tbsp
  • Pure vanilla extract | 1 tsp
  • Water | 1 cup
  • Chocolate chips | 1 cup

Steps

  1. Pre-heat the oven to 400 degree F or 200 degree C for at least 10 minutes
  2. Prepare the muffin tray, line it with muffin liners or spray with nonstick cooking spray or lightly butter it
  3. In a bowl, take flour, sugar, cocoa powder, baking powder, baking soda and salt
  4. Mix them well. If your cocoa powder has many lumps then don't forget to sift it.
  5. In another bowl add oil, vanilla, vinegar and water.
  6. Whisk it very well.
  7. Then add dry mixture
  8. Whisk it till it gets incorporated well. Do not over beat it.
  9. Add chocolate chips
  10. Switch to spatula and fold them in.
  11. Divide the batter in to prepared 12 muffins cups
  12. Sprinkle some chocolate chips on top. I am adding here so it looks better, if I am making it on regular basis for my family I wouldn't bother for this step.
  13. Bake into pre-heated oven for 20-22 minutes. Check by inserting a toothpick into the center and if it comes out clean then muffins are ready. Remove it from the oven; let them cook in a pan for 5 minutes.
  14. After that it is cool to touch, remove them on cooling rack to cool completely.
