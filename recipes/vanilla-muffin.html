Vanilla Muffin

Eggless Recipe

Ingredients

  • Refined Flour| 190 gm
  • Castor Sugar | 130 gm
  • Baking Powder | 1/4 tsp
  • Baking Soda | 3/4 tsp
  • Salt | 1/4 tsp
  • Milk | 180 gm
  • Oil | 80 ml
  • Vanilla | 1/2 tsp
  • Vinegar | 1 tsp

Steps

  1. Line the cupcake tray with cupcake liners. If using a cake pan, line it with a parchment circle
  2. Sieve and mix all dry ingredients, refined flour, baking soda, baking powder, and salt except sugar in a bowl
  3. Add vinegar to milk and let it stand for a minute
  4. Add brown sugar to milk and vinegar mixture and whisk for 1 minute until the sugar is almost dissolved
  5. Now add in oil. Whip the liquid ingredients until the oil is wholly blended well
  6. Mix liquid in the dry ingredients and with the help of whisk mix well so that no lumps are there
  7. Bake in a preheated oven at 180 Â°C for about 18-20 minutes
  8. Insert a toothpick in the centre of the muffins; if it comes out clean, it is ready
  9. Remove it from the oven and rest in a cool place.
